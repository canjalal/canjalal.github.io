About

I got here by following curiosity. It started with lasers — measuring molecular signals at the limits of detection — then moved through classrooms, and eventually landed me in production systems reaching millions of people. The through-line is finding elegant solutions to problems that matter to someone.

At Tort Experts I led a team that delivered measurable business impact. At CommonLit I helped build tools teachers and students depend on daily. Both felt like the same work: understand a complex system, then make it better for the humans on the other side.

I care about code that the next engineer will be glad to inherit, products that work for everyone, and teams where people feel safe enough to say what they don't know. I co-led documentation and QA overhauls at two companies — because good systems need good foundations.

Outside of work I'm usually biking around San Francisco, learning a language (fluent in English and Spanish, working on Hebrew), or facilitating community workshops. I'm drawn to work where the product's success is someone's better day.
